Privacy Policy

Your privacy — and your child's privacy — is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account data: name and email address when you sign in with Google.
  • Child profile data: the child's first name, age group, gender, and language preference — provided by you to personalise stories.
  • Child photo / avatar: a photo you upload to create the child's illustrated avatar. This image is used solely for generating the avatar. Your child's photo is deleted as soon as the avatar is generated. If not deleted immediately due to processing, it is automatically removed within 30 days.
  • Story content: story text and generated illustrations, stored to let you download your storybook PDF.
  • Payment data: payment is processed by Razorpay. We do not store card details. We retain order identifiers and amounts for billing records.
  • Usage data: anonymous analytics (page views, feature interactions) to improve the product.

2. Children's Data

Tingu Tales does not knowingly collect personal data directly from children. All child profile information is provided by a parent or guardian. We do not sell, share, or use children's data for advertising purposes. The child's photo is used exclusively to generate the illustrated avatar and is never shared with third parties. Once the avatar is generated, we ask you to delete the photo from the app immediately. If not deleted by you, the original photo is automatically deleted within 30 days of upload. The generated avatar illustration (not the original photo) is retained to maintain consistency across story pages.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To generate personalised storybooks.
  • To send you your completed storybook and transactional emails.
  • To process payments and manage refunds.
  • To improve our systems and product experience using anonymised and aggregated data. We do not use your child's photos to train AI models.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with trusted service providers who process it on our behalf (Google Cloud, Firebase, Razorpay, Google Gemini / Imagen AI APIs). Each provider is contractually obligated to protect your data.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. You may request deletion at any time. Generated storybook images and PDFs are retained to allow re-downloads.

Child photo: the original photo you upload is automatically deleted as soon as the avatar is generated, or within 30 days at most. The generated avatar illustration is kept separately and is not the original photo.

6. Security

Data is stored on Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Access is restricted to authorised personnel and automated systems only.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you or your child. Email us at hello@achint.dev and we will respond within 7 business days.

Refund Policy

When you are eligible for a refund

We want you to be completely happy with your storybook. If we are unable to generate a completed storybook PDF due to a technical failure on our end, you are entitled to a full refund.

How to request a refund

Use the "Request Refund" option within the app on the relevant story, or email us at hello@achint.dev within 7 days of your purchase. Include your order reference and a brief description of the issue.

Processing time

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days back to your original payment method via Razorpay.

Non-refundable situations

  • A completed storybook PDF has already been generated and downloaded successfully.
  • The request is made more than 7 days after purchase.
  • The issue was caused by incorrect information (e.g., wrong child name or language) provided during story creation.

Content Policy

All content generated by Tingu Tales is designed to be safe, age-appropriate, and culturally respectful for children. Our AI systems are configured to:

  • Produce positive, educational, and emotionally enriching stories.
  • Celebrate Indian culture, traditions, and languages without stereotyping.
  • Never generate violent, sexually explicit, hateful, or harmful content.
  • Avoid inclusion of brand names, political figures, or controversial real-world topics.

Every generated story goes through an automated quality review. If you believe a generated story contains inappropriate content, please report it immediately at hello@achint.dev and we will investigate and take corrective action.

Users must not attempt to manipulate the story creation inputs to bypass these safeguards. Accounts found intentionally bypassing safety safeguards may be suspended, and refunds may not be provided.
